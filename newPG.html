ABOUT US



At "SAGE - the picture gallery"

we are showcasing the breathtaking beauty and awe-inspiring wonders of nature through the lens of photography. Our gallery is a haven for nature enthusiasts, capturing the essence of the natural world and allowing you to experience its magnificence wherever you may be. Here you will find a diverse range of nature photography. Each image has been thoughtfully selected to evoke a sense of wonder, serenity, and connection to the natural world. This website has been created flaunt and appreciate the photography skills of Ms. Sejal Sharma, Mr. Dhruv Punetha, and Mr. Aryan Rana. Through their lenses, they have immortalized fleeting moments, dramatic vistas, and intricate details, encapsulating the essence of our planet's extraordinary beauty.
